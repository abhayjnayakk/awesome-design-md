# Contributing

Thanks for improving DESIGN.md Style Gallery.

## What To Contribute

Good contributions include:

- Better taxonomy explanations.
- New public links to useful DESIGN.md resources.
- Original starter examples.
- Prompt improvements.
- Corrections to attribution or usage guidance.

## What Not To Contribute

Please do not submit:

- Exact brand clones.
- Logos, proprietary assets, or copied marketing copy.
- Private design systems you do not own.
- Full mirrors of another collection without a clear reason.

## Starter Example Guidelines

Starter examples should be:

- Original and generic.
- Useful for a product category.
- Written as `DESIGN.md`.
- Clear enough for an agent to apply.
- Not named after a real brand.

## Pull Request Checklist

- The contribution is original or properly attributed.
- Links work.
- The writing is practical for AI builders.
- Examples avoid trademarked names and copied brand identity.
