# Remix Safely

Use references as design vocabulary, not brand cloning material.

## Safe To Borrow

- Density
- Spacing rhythm
- Contrast level
- Reading flow
- Component priority
- Motion restraint
- Surface hierarchy
- Documentation rhythm

## Do Not Copy

- Logos
- Brand names in your product UI
- Exact page layouts
- Marketing copy
- Proprietary illustrations
- Signature icons
- A complete competitor identity

## Remix Formula

```text
[Primary reference] for [structure]
[Secondary reference] for [tone]
[Your product] for [component needs]
```

Example:

```text
Vercel for developer precision, Claude for editorial warmth, and our AI compliance product for evidence and audit components.
```

## Originality Checklist

- The final `DESIGN.md` has your own product name.
- Token names are generic or product-specific.
- At least one major dimension differs from the reference: color, type, layout, shape, or motion.
- The UI solves your users' job, not the reference brand's job.
- The design works without mentioning the inspiration.
